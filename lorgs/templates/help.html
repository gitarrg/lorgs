We have three main sections:

  • Spell Selection
  • Navigation Bar
  • Timeline View

Timeline

Each cast can have these elements:

Cast Time

Indicates the exact time when this spell was cast.

Duration

Shows for how long a spell has been active (when applicable).

Cooldown

How long a spell has been on cooldown.
In some cases (for spells that have their cooldowns reduced by talents, legendaries or other effect) the value here is an estimate.

Click on any Cast to focus it and all instances of the same spell.

Ctrl+Click to focus multiple spells at once.

Click and Drag on the timeline-ticks or anywhere on the background to move.

Boss Lane

The Boss-Casts are taken from the #1 Log!

Based on the boss-encounter and guilds dps/pushtimings/strat those timings might vary from slightly from kill to kill.

Keep that in mind when you ask yourself why seemingly some players use their CD's a few seconds before or after an important ability. This can most likely be due to that guild getting said boss ability a few seconds earlier/later.

Remember that you can click on the icons in the yellow box, to show/hide abilities.

Player Names

Click on any name to open the corresponding log on warcraftlogs.

Timeline Ruler

to aid you check timings across all parses, not just the first few,
we added a little bar, that can be dragged around.

Spells Toolbar

Display-Group

the Display Group offers you three buttons to customize the overall view:

Cast Time

show/hide the time stamp for each cast

Duration

show/hide for how long each cast was active.
eg.: Healing Tide Totem is active for 10sec.

Cooldown

show/hide spell cooldowns.
Toggle the display for the cooldown-bars, that indicate for how long the spell was on cooldown. This can be useful to see if players decided to hold a spell, or used it right as it came of cooldown.

Spells/Consumables/Items:

next you have icons for each spell/consumable/trinket that has been found in those logs.

Click on any spell to show/hide it.
Click on a Group-Name to toggle all spells in that group.

These are roughly split into 4 categories:

Boss:

abilities used by the encounter

Spec:

spells cast by the player(s)

Potions:

pots and consumables

Trinkets:

on-use trinkets and similar items
