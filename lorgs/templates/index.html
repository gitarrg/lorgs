What is this?

This little website looks at the top 50 logs for each spec
and visualizes relevant(*) spells on a timeline.

The Idea is to give players a quick overview how to time their CDs, without having to go trough a number of logs themself and compare them by hand.

Help

⚠️Disclaimer

Do not simply copy paste the timings you see here!

This page is meant to give you a guideline,
but you'll have to adapt your cooldowns based on your guilds strat, setup, push timings and many many more variables.
